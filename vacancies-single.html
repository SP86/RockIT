SENIOR LOW-CODE/ COREZOID DEVELOPER

Remote Low-code/Corezoid Software Developer

НЕОБХІДНІ НАВИЧКИ

Low-code development / strong Building APIs / strong Cloud architecture / good JavaScript / good BPM / good We are looking for a Senior Low-code/Corezoid Developer (Corezoid platform and other tools) to build modern digital products ranging from chatbots to complex cloud systems for neo banks. You will participate in the creation of web systems, mobile apps, and cloud platforms at all stages – communication with the client, technical design, implementation, and support. We are in need for an experienced engineer with strong communication skills capable of talking with a team and clients in technical and business languages.

TEAM:

Digitalization Program is a team of creative engineers working with a mix of modern low-code approaches based on Corezoid and other tools as well as proven industry leading technologies (.NET, PHP, JavaScript) on the web, mobile, and cloud. We help our customers build communication platforms, marketplaces, fintech products, and much more. JOINING THE TEAM, YOU WILL GET A CHANCE TO: Work with modern and rapidly evolving projects that help businesses digitalize processes Develop products in trending domains Talk to clients in Ukraine and Western Europe, see your projects go live fast

ВИМОГИ

4+ years of professional experience as a software engineer 1+ years of experience in low-code development Strong understanding of web services (REST, SOAP) and best practices, experience in building REST APIs, various authentication and authorization approaches Experience with JavaScript, JSON, and NO SQL databases Experience in Agile software development processes (Scrum, Kanban) At least Intermediate level of English or higher

WILL BE A PLUS:

Experience working with Corezoid or other business process automation systems Experience in database design and development (SQL Server, PostgreSQL, MySQL) Knowledge of Angular or React Front-end development, JavaScript, markup (HTML, CSS) Working knowledge with Git tool and other repositories Experience in working with Amazon Web Services, Azure, or other cloud platforms Cloud services understanding (AWS, Azure) will be an advantage Readiness to show examples of source code and developed apps

ПРОФЕСІЙНИЙ ПРОФАЙЛ

Strong communication and analytical skills Self-management, flexibility Solution-oriented way of thinking Creative approach to problem solving Good teamwork and collaboration ОБОВ'ЯЗКИ Develop and deploy digital tools & apps: websites, chatbots, CRM systems, integration interfaces and databases Use low-code approaches with Corezoid ecosystem and other tools Analyze and troubleshoot issues, support your applications Work with colleagues and stakeholders as a part of the full product development life cycle Perform code review, prepare technical documentation Take part in daily standups and other syncs with customer Perform demos for clients Report status and progress according to the project and company demands
